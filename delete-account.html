Hello Stranger Account & Data Deletion

We believe in giving you full control over your data. You can permanently delete your Hello Stranger account and all associated data at any time from directly within the app.

How to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the Hello Stranger app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the "Settings" tab (the gear icon).
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page.
  4. Tap the "Delete Account" button.
  5. A confirmation pop-up will appear. You must confirm this action to proceed.

What Data is Deleted?

Upon confirmation, all of your personal information will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems. This includes:

  • Your account (email, username, and encrypted password).
  • Your profile (level, XP, and current streak).
  • Your entire quest history and log.
  • All of your earned achievements.
  • Any progress in Prestige Paths.
  • Any endgame quests you may have submitted as a Mentor.
